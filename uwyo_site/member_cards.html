• WyCS

    The Hub for Computer Science Outreach in Wyoming

    A partnership between SEED in the College of Education and CEDAR in the College of Engineering and Applied Science. 

    SEED is directed by Dr. Andrea Burrows (Andrea.Burrows@uwyo.edu) and
    CEDAR is directed by Dr. Mike Borowczak (Mike.Borowczak@uwyo.edu)

    WyCS is a collaboration with support from the National Science Foundation, Wyoming Department of Education, Wyoming Professional Teaching Standards Board, National Security Agency, Microsoft, MilliporeSigma, and the University of Wyoming. Any views and ideas expressed here are those of authors, and not necessarily those of any funding organization or agency.

    A cybersecurity professional development for up to 10 teachers and a summer camp for 40-50 students. More details about registration coming soon! Student and teacher participants receive microbits and access to other resources, while teacher participants also receive a stipend.

    Student Camp: June 24/25 (Virtual) + Sept. 6th-Oct 10th, 2021 (Schools Throughout Wyoming)
    Showcase: Oct 11th-15th. 2021 / Location: Virtual

    Who can sign up? Any K-12 teacher! Teachers must recruit 4-5 local students to participate (2 half days in June, virtually; +30 hrs in early Fall) - send Mike.Borowczak@uwyo.edu an email.
